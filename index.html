About Sigma0

Founded by Alan Wilson-Langman , Sigma0 is a research-driven radar technology consultancy helping companies develop advanced sensing solutions.

With a Ph.D. in Electronic Engineering specializing in radar systems, Alan brings over 25 years of expertise in radar and RF sensor development. His experience spans from pioneering work at industry leaders like John Deere and Joby Aviation to groundbreaking research in SAR technology and autonomous systems.

At Sigma0, we combine deep technical knowledge with practical implementation experience to deliver cutting-edge radar solutions. Our expertise covers the complete spectrum of radar technology - from system architecture and RF design to advanced signal processing and real-world deployment.
